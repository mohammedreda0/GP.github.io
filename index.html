VR PROJECT

The projectâ€™s goal is to create a VR gaming platform using the mirror therapy effect. VR has the ability to overcome the drawbacks of traditional treatment in the field of stroke rehabilitation. Because the upper limb is involved in most ADLs, improving upper limb (UL) function is essential in stroke deficits. The game is anticipated to contain a number of therapy modules that suit patients of various ages, from children to elderly people. The game will offer a range of levels from basic to advanced for each age group. This program is expected to aid in regaining the function of joints whose mobility has been compromised by subacute stroke or its consequences.
